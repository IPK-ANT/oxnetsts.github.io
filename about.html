About OxNet STS

OxNet STS is an open network connecting researchers, students, and practitioners interested in Science & Technology Studies across the University of Oxford and beyond.

Our Mission

  • Foster interdisciplinary dialogue between STEM, social sciences, and humanities.
  • Showcase Oxford STS research to wider academic, policy, and public audiences.
  • Provide a platform for events, workshops, and collaborative grants.

Who We Are

OxNet is run by a volunteer steering group drawn from departments including the School of Anthropology, Geography, History of Science, Sociology, and the Oxford Internet Institute.
